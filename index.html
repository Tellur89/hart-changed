About Us

Whether you just fancy a pub lunch or breakfast, have a meeting with business colleagues for coffee, are planning a special night or simply having drinks with friends and family, the White Hart, Little Waltham is the perfect choice.

Modern pub food and seasonal specials are all made from scratch using the finest, freshest ingredients & can be enjoyed alongside thirst quenching drinks, wines & cocktails.

Located in the beautiful village of Little Waltham, close to Chelmsford, the pub has been refurbished to provide a stylish yet relaxed environment suitable for everyone. The beautiful garden, heated Marquee & outside space can be used throughout the year for al fresco dining & drinking & customers are welcomed to use the online ordering system either indoors or outside; it couldn't be easier to enjoy a safe visit out.

The White Hart holds Weekly Offers, the best Sunday Roasts, Live Music, Children's Menu, Beer Festivals & more making it the ideal family local.

Loyalty cards


Why not join our fantastic Loyalty Scheme available to use at any of our venues?

All you need to do is register your details with us either over the phone or next time you visit. To receive your points, use your registered contact information when booking online, in person or when purchasing food & drink.

For every £1 you spend you will receive 5p in points to spend on any food & drink which you can redeem at any time up to £100.
